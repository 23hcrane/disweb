Disneyland Facts

Disneyland is my favorite theme park to go to with my family! I have gone almost every year since I was little.



  • Here are six facts about Disneyland!
    1. Disneyland was first opened in 1955.
    2. Upon opening day in 1955, 28,000 people attended, however half of those purchased forged tickets or snuck into the park by hopping the fence.

    3. Each year, there are 2.8 million churros sold at Disneyland.

    4. The most popular ride in Disneyland is Pirates of the Caribbean.

      • This ride was orginally meant to be a wax museum.
      • This ride is the longest one in the park taking a total of 15 minutes.
      • The Disneyland version of this ride uses some real human bones.
      • This was the last attraction that Walt Disney personally oversaw.
    5. There are hidden Mickey's found all over the park. Here are some of those places:
      • Alice in Wonderland- In the scene where the Queen of Hearts are painting the roses red, one of the roses is a hidden Mickey.
      • Haunted Mansion- In the grand ballroom scene, the plate table settings resemble a Mickey Mouse head.
      • Mr. Toad's Wild Ride- On the toad statue in the queue of the ride, you can see a small hidden Mickey in the statue's eye.
    6. Main Street uses forced perspective to make it feel grander!
